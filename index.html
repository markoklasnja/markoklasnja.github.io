Welcome. I am a post-doctoral fellow at the Center for the Study of Democratic Politics at Princeton University, specializing in comparative politics, political behavior, and political economy of democratic accountability. In the Fall 2015, I will be joining the faculty of the Government Department and the Edmund A. Walsh School of Foreign Service at Georgetown University as an Assistant Professor.
